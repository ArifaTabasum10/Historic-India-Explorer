About Pinjore Gardens

Also known as Yadavindra Gardens, Pinjore Gardens is a historic 17th-century Mughal garden located in the town of Pinjore in Haryana. Designed on the classical Charbagh pattern, the garden features terraces, fountains, water channels, and pavilions.

These gardens are a popular tourist destination known for their well-maintained greenery and beautiful architecture. Cultural festivals and light shows are also hosted here throughout the year.
