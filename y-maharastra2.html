About the Bibi ka Maqbara

The Bibi Ka Maqbara (English: "Tomb of the Lady"[1][2]) is a tomb located in the city of Aurangabad in the Indian state of Maharashtra

It was commissioned in 1660 by the Mughal emperor Aurangzeb's son, Prince Azam Shah, in the memory of his mother Dilras Banu Begum (posthumously known as Rabia-ul-Durrani).
