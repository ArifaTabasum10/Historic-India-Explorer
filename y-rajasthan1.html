About Galtaji Temple

Galtaji is an ancient Hindu pilgrimage site located in the Aravalli Hills near Jaipur. It is known for its natural springs, sacred water tanks, and a series of temples dedicated to the Sun God, Lord Hanuman, and Lord Rama.

The temple complex is famous for its natural water tanks, or kunds, where pilgrims take ritual baths. It is also home to many monkeys, earning it the nickname "Monkey Temple."
