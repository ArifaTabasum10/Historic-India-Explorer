About the Padmanabhaswamy Temple

It is one of the 108 Divya Desams, considered the sacred abodes of Vishnu in the Sri Vaishnava tradition. Adi Shankara had created sacred hymns on Anantha Padmanabha and it is an important holy site for Smartha Tradition. The name of the city in Malayalam and Tamil translates to "The City of Ananta" (Ananta being a form of Vishnu).
