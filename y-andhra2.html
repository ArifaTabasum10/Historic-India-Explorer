About Tirupati Balaji Temple

The Tirupati Balaji Temple, also known as the Venkateswara Temple, is one of the most visited and revered temples in India. It is located on the Tirumala hills in Andhra Pradesh and is dedicated to Lord Venkateswara, an incarnation of Lord Vishnu.

This ancient temple is known for its spiritual significance, Dravidian architecture, and the tradition of tonsure (hair offering). Pilgrims from across the world visit the temple to seek blessings and donate generously, making it one of the wealthiest religious institutions in the world.
